About WETRACK

WETRACK is a state-of-the-art web-based application designed to revolutionize the monitoring of ex-prisoners, particularly parolees and probationers. Our system empowers authorized personnel from POLRI, LAPAS, and BAPAS with advanced tools to manage and track individuals under supervision. By harnessing the power of real-time location tracking, intelligent geo-fencing, and AI-driven compliance management features, WETRACK is at the forefront of enhancing public safety while supporting the successful reintegration of monitored individuals into society.
